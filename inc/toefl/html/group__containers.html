enum toefl::bc

Possible boundary conditions for the ghostmatrix class. Naming follows FFTW R2R kind naming.

See also
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discrete_sine_transform
Enumerator
TL_PERIODIC 

Periodic boundary.

TL_DST00 

dst (discrete sine transform) 1

TL_DST10 

dst 2

TL_DST01 

dst 3

TL_DST11 

dst 4

enum toefl::Padding

Provide various padding types of the Matrix class.

Enumerator
TL_NONE 

Don't use any padding.

TL_DFT 

Pad lines with 2-cols%2 elements for inplace DFT in horizontal direction.

TL_DRT_DFT 

Add two 2-rows%2 lines at end of matrix for inplace DFT in vertical direction.
